Why Do Plants Get Disease

Plants, like all living organisms, can get diseases. These diseases can be caused by various pathogens, including fungi, bacteria, viruses, and nematodes. Understanding the types of infections, reasons behind them, and how to prevent these problems is crucial for maintaining healthy plants.
